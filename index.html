About Us

We are a non-profit charitable trust dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of those in need. Since our inception in 2003, we have been working tirelessly to provide education, cow welfare, and other charitable services to the communities in Mithapur, Dwarka, Gujarat.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide educational opportunities for children, promote cow welfare, and support underserved communities. We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and creating a brighter future for children. We also believe that cows are gentle and intelligent creatures that deserve to be treated with respect and compassion. We are committed to working with communities to improve the lives of cows and promote sustainable cow care practices.
