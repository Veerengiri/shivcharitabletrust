About Us

Shree Shiv Education and Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization committed to positively impacting the lives of those in need. Since our inception in 2003, we have tirelessly provided education, cow welfare, Samuhik Vivah, healthcare, and other charitable services to the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people through education, compassion, and support. As a non-profit trust, we provide scholarships, quality education, healthcare, and community-focused initiatives like Samuhik Vivah. Our cow welfare programs complement our holistic approach to uplifting lives, a commitment we have upheld since 2003.
